Impacts Of Mining.

Why do We Mine?

People mine for many reasons, but the most important reason is to extract necessary materials for construction. In our society, almost everything is created from the materials that we gathered from the Earth. Buildings, roads, machinery, and other things that benefits our life are supported by mining resources from this planet.

Without mining, we would be running out of resources all the time to create these essential products in society. However, is this really worth the damage we've caused to the environment, and is there a better way to mine instead?
